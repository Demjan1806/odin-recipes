Greek Chicken Gyros

Description

Personally, my favorite cuisine is the Greek cuisine. So I have to put a Greek recipe in here. This Greek chicken gyros is covered in delicious tzatziki and wrapped in a nice crispy pita.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken thigh fillets
  • 3 large garlic cloves
  • 3 tbsp lemon juice
  • 3 tbsp Greek yogurt
  • 1.5 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • tzatziki
  • 4 to 6 pita breads

Steps

  1. Place the Marinade ingredients in a ziplock bag and massage to mix. Add the chicken into the ziplock bag and massage to cover all the chicken in the Marinade. Marinate for at least 2 hours, preferably 3 hours, ideally 12 hours and no longer than 24 hours.
  2. Brush the outdoor grill with oil, then preheat on medium high. Or heat 1 tbsp of oil in a fry pan over medium high heat.
  3. Remove chicken from Marinade. Cook the chicken for 2 to 3 minutes on each side, until golden brown and cooked through (cooking time depends on size of chicken).
  4. Remove the chicken from the grill / fry pan onto a plate. Cover loosely with foil and allow to rest for 5 minutes before serving.
  5. If your chicken thighs are large, you may need to cut them. Mine were small.
  6. Get a pita bread or flatbread (preferably warmed) and place it on a piece of parchment (baking) paper (or you could use foil). Place some Salad down the middle of the bread, then top with chicken and Tzatziki.
  7. Roll the wrap up, enclosing it with the parchment paper. Twist the end with the excess parchment paper to secure it and cut if desired.
  8. I prefer to lay everything out on a table and let everyone help themselves.
